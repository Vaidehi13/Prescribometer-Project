About Us

What do we do?

Our system is designed for doctors & physicians for generating accurate & time saving electronic prescriptions. After complete examination of the patient, the doctor can dictate the prescription including symptoms, medicines and suggestions to our system. Prescribo-meter will generate a meticulous and accurate prescription in seconds & the prescription can also be emailed directly to the patient on their registered email-id.

What do you get?

You can save your time & make prescriptions effortless. We also provide highly secure digital signature facility to help you generate prescriptions faster, efficient & go paperless! You can easily access patient's previous medical history with the help of our system. We respect your privacy & provide you data confidentiality to enhance your data privacy.
